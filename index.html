About Our Bakery and Café

We offer a wide and ever changing variety of cakes from our bakery, freshly made in house using the highest quality ingredients. You'll find pretty cupcakes, lovely loaf cakes, delectable handcrafted petit fours and slices of our signature layer cakes or gateaux waiting for you to enjoy in our café with a hot or cold drink, or to take away with you and enjoy at home.


We also offer our signature cakes and gateaux as whole sharing cakes suitable for any celebration. Visit us to find out what's available or contact us to enquire or make a special request. We have a dedicated team who are always happy to help.

Bespoke Celebration Cakes

We create luxury celebration cakes to your specific requirements. Our cakes are sure to impress, created with mouth-watering flavours, expert craftsmanship and acute attention to detail. Every element of your cake will be handcrafted using the finest ingredients.


We can help you in the design process to give you the cake of your dreams. Whether you would like an elaborate themed cake or fine detailed pipework, we will create a delicious masterpiece for your special occasion.
